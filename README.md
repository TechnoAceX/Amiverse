# Amiverse

Amiverse is a student platform built for **Amity University Rajasthan** to simplify campus life through a single digital ecosystem. It brings together announcements, events, academic resources, internships, clubs, and student engagement, making university information more accessible and organized.

---

## 📖 About

Amiverse was developed to provide students with a centralized platform for everything related to campus life. By reducing fragmented communication across multiple channels, it helps students stay informed, connected, and engaged throughout their academic journey.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📢 Announcements & Events
Access official university announcements, campus news, and upcoming events.

### 📝 Student Community
Share posts, ideas, and discussions with fellow students.

### 💼 Internships & Academic Resources
Discover internship opportunities and access study materials in one place.

### 🔍 Lost & Found
Report and recover lost items through a dedicated portal.

### 🎭 Clubs & Activities
Explore student clubs, societies, and campus events.

### 🚀 Projects & Leaderboard
Showcase projects and highlight student achievements.

### 👤 Student Profiles
Connect with classmates and build your campus network.

### 🛠️ Upcoming Features
- Campus Snap Map
- Interactive University Map
- Attendance Tracker

---

## 🎯 Vision

Amiverse aims to create a connected and collaborative campus environment where students can easily access information, discover opportunities, and engage with the university community through a single platform.

---

## 📌 Project Status

Amiverse is currently under active development. New features, performance improvements, and user experience enhancements are continuously being added to expand the platform and improve the overall student experience.
